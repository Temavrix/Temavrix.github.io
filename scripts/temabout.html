ABOUT Temavrix

We love coding

About our organisation

Temavrix is a brand founded and created on 2019 primarily as a youtube gaming channel. Soon the small scale startup moved into the coding world and established a base in github to post free, open-source repositories for the world to see. Our aim is to bring an end to many problems both in the computer industry and the real world through open-source projects by innovating and solving problems. We have worked on a very few small-scaled projects so far with a constant hope that we can help the world in a small positive way. While we still have some ongoing and some ambitious projects coming up we will like to ask you to support our startup by simply visiting our github projects and contributing to them by starring and reporting a issue to help fix bugs and mistakes and improve our projects for others.

What we do in Temavrix?

Here at Temavrix we usually make programs coded in python, C++ and also do web design using html and css. We mostly spend time developing products by creating, testing and deploying our products and then also improve the design and code optimisation for our projects and also spend time reviewing codes for all our products (including this website) to make sure it is updated to today's standards and informations available. We also learn new things as we code with the help of stackoverflow and other helpful websites as references. Temavrix may have a few projects but we hope that it has helped people from different walks of life. To innovate and advance technology we know today well it might be hard but everyday new achievments and milestones in coding logic, practices and habits are being made, so why not be a part of it here at Temavrix by contributing to our open-source projects over at github.
Note: As of 1st May 2023 all of Temavrix Systems have been upgraded to Windows 11 to keep up with current industrial standards.

Cycle Of Development for our projects:-

CYCLE OF DEVELOPMENT (PRE-DEPLOYMENT):- Creating Product --> Testing Product --> Initial Beta Run--> Deploying Product
CYCLE OF DEVELOPMENT (POST-DEPLOYMENT):- Product Deployed --> Advertise Product --> Fix Important Bugs and Issues From Users
CYCLE OF DEVELOPMENT (PRE-END-OF-SUPPORT (EOS)):- Bugs and Issues Fixed --> Monthly Updates And improvements --> Product Is Deprecated (EOS)
